Welcome to my website
I make stylised, expressive, figurative sculptures of animals in clay which make people smile and are very collectable.I enjoy creating something a bit different every time and there is a tale behind the making of every piece conceived and brought into being in my Yorkshire workshop.Working from personal observations I research my ideas further in books and on the internet for detail and background material. I find it enriching to discuss my work with the public even using some anecdotes to inspire new works. I have exhibited and sold my work throughout the UK since 1994. Please browse the gallery to enjoy examples from the full range of my work.

Please visit the online gallery to view further examples of a growing menagerie and dramatic sculptures.
